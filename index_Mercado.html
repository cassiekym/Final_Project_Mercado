About Us


Kash Figure Store is an online store composed of a passionate figure collector who aspires to promote awareness in supporting authentic figures and collectibles, thus buying from us assures you that you're getting the real deal!


At Kash Figure Store, our core principle has always been to deliver happiness to hobbyists which can only be attained by attending and delivering customer needs and offering or selling only authentic items at a reasonable price.

What are Nendoroids?


The Nendoroid series features cute, chibi-style figures that fit in the palm of your hand. They can always be by your side to support you throughout the hustle and bustle of everyday life.
